L'Histoire

Notre maison, notre passion.

ALTESSE was born from a singular conviction: that the most powerful thing a woman can wear is the dress that makes her feel invincible.

2020
ALTESSE founded in a small atelier on Rue Cambon. Three artisans, one sewing machine, and a vision of couture that honors both tradition and the modern woman.
2022
"Celestial Dreams" — the debut collection — debuts at a private salon show. Twelve gowns. Twelve standing ovations.
2024
The atelier expands to seven petites mains. ALTESSE gowns begin appearing on red carpets from Cannes to the Met Gala.
2026
"Brides de Legende" redefines bridal couture. Every ALTESSE bride receives a gown that is not just worn, but inhabited.
